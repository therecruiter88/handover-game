How to Play

Cross the glass bridge by choosing the correct path with left and right arrow keys.

Each row has two glass panels - one is tempered and will hold your weight, the other will break!

Make your way across all the 8 tempered panels to win.

Be careful! You only have 3 lives and a 30 second time limit!
